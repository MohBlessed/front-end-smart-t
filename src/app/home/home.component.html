About NAMé Recycling

NAMé recycling is the realization of a dream from two young entrepreneurs – Roblain Namegni & Thomas Poelmans. Their dream became reality in 2016 when they started a plastic recycling operations in Limbé, Cameroon. The mission of NAMé Recycling is to promote circulair economy by giving a second life to plastic waste and reducing plastic pollution while bringing economic opportunities to the communities we serve. NAMé Recycling’s aim is to reduce the amount of waste by recycling and reintroducing these materials into the value chain, contributing to a circular economy.
